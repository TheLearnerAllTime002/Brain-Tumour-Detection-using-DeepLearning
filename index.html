Risk Levels & Possible Cures
  • High risk: Possible malignancy—urgent care. Treatments: Surgery, radiotherapy, chemotherapy
  • Medium risk: Review with doctor soon. Treatments: Often similar, prognosis better
  • Low risk: Likely benign, follow up always required.
  • No tumor: All clear. Consult doctor if symptoms persist.
